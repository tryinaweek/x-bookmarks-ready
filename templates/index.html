How It Works

Sign up with Google or email above. Your personal dashboard is created instantly with a private Cloud Firestore database for your bookmarks.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is X-Bookmarks?
X-Bookmarks is a Chrome extension and web dashboard that syncs your X (formerly Twitter) bookmarks into a private, AI-powered knowledge base. It automatically categorizes your saves by topic, tracks your research timeline, and helps you repurpose saved content into new posts.
How does X-Bookmarks use AI?
X-Bookmarks uses Claude AI by Anthropic to analyze and categorize your bookmarks. The AI reads each bookmark's text, identifies the topic and key themes, groups related saves together, and can draft new content (tweets, threads, summaries) using your bookmarks as source material.
Is my X/Twitter data private?
Yes. Your bookmarks are synced directly from your browser to your private Cloud Firestore database via encrypted HTTPS. There are no middleman servers. We do not sell, share, or access your data. You can delete all your data at any time from the dashboard settings.
Do I need a Twitter/X API developer account?
No. X-Bookmarks works by reading bookmarks directly from your browser page, so you don't need API keys, developer tokens, or any special X/Twitter account access. Just install the extension and sync.
Is X-Bookmarks free?
X-Bookmarks offers a free tier that includes bookmark syncing and basic dashboard access. AI-powered features like smart categorization, content drafting, and advanced analytics use AI credits. Premium plans are available for heavy users who need unlimited AI processing.
What browsers does X-Bookmarks support?
X-Bookmarks currently supports Google Chrome via a Chrome Web Store extension. The web dashboard works in any modern browser. Support for Firefox and other Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Arc) is planned.
How many bookmarks can X-Bookmarks sync?
There is no hard limit on the number of bookmarks you can sync. The extension scrolls through your X bookmarks page and captures everything visible. Users have successfully synced collections of over 5,000 bookmarks.
Can I export my bookmarks?
Yes. X-Bookmarks lets you export your entire bookmark collection as an Excel spreadsheet from the dashboard, including the AI-generated categories and metadata.
