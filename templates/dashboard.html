Welcome to MyBookmarks!

To unlock AI-powered categorization, content suggestions, and bookmark intelligence, let's sync your Twitter/X bookmarks.

🔌

Method 1: Chrome Extension Helper (Recommended)

Sync bookmarks directly from your browser in one click. Bypasses all Twitter developer API fees and limits.

Setup Instructions:
  1. Install the X-Bookmarks Chrome Extension directly from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Go to x.com/i/bookmarks.
  3. Click the floating Sync Bookmarks panel on the bottom-right to link and synchronize your bookmarks.
No X Dev Account Required
